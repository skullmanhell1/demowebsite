FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Common questions patients ask before their first appointment.

Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?
No referral is needed to book with us directly. We are happy to work alongside referrals for insurers where required.
Do you offer private health rebates and HICAPS?
Yes. On-the-spot HICAPS claiming is available so you only pay the gap on the day of your appointment.
What should I wear to my appointment?
Wear comfortable clothing you can move freely in, so we can assess and treat the area properly.
Do you treat sports injuries and post-surgery rehab?
Yes. We build tailored programs for sports injuries, post-operative rehabilitation and return-to-activity goals.
How long is a typical session?
Initial assessments run around 45 minutes, with follow-up treatment sessions usually around 30 minutes.
Can I claim through workers comp or DVA?
Yes. We manage approved workers compensation and DVA claims so you can focus on recovery.
