Questions & answers

Everything you need before booking.

Clear answers reduce phone calls, cancellations and hesitation — and reassure new patients before their first visit.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

No referral is needed for most services. You can book dentistry, chiropractic, med spa, optometry or physiotherapy directly. For certain rebates or specialist care, a referral may help — our reception team will let you know when it applies.

Which health funds and payment options do you accept?

We support HICAPS on-the-spot private health claiming, Medicare-eligible items, major cards and interest-free payment plans for larger treatment. You will always receive a clear estimate before treatment begins.

What happens at a first appointment?

Your first visit is consultation-led: we review your history and goals, complete any needed assessment, and explain findings and options in plain language. You decide on next steps with no pressure — nothing is booked or charged without your agreement.

How do I reschedule or cancel a booking?

You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment at no cost, using the link in your confirmation message or by calling reception on 08 6240 4440. We send reminders by SMS and email so appointments are easy to keep.

Do you offer same-week or urgent appointments?

Yes — we hold same-week slots across most disciplines and aim to respond to every request within 24 hours. For genuine medical emergencies, please call 000 or attend your nearest emergency department.

Is my personal and health information kept private?

Absolutely. Records are stored securely and handled in line with Australian privacy and health-records requirements. Your information is only shared with your care team or as you direct.
