FAQ

Frequently asked questions

The details clients check before booking their next set.

How long does a gel manicure last?
A gel manicure usually lasts two to three weeks, depending on nail growth and how hard your hands work day to day.
Do you take walk-ins or is it appointment only?
Appointments are recommended so we can hold the right time for you. Limited walk-ins are welcome subject to availability.
How do I safely remove gel or acrylic?
Book a professional soak-off rather than peeling product away, which protects the health of your natural nail.
Do you offer nail art and custom designs?
Yes, from subtle single-nail accents through to detailed custom sets. Bring inspiration photos and we'll tailor a look.
Are your tools sterilised between clients?
Absolutely. We use hospital-grade sterilisation for reusable tools and single-use files and buffers for every client.
What is your cancellation policy?
We appreciate at least 24 hours notice so we can offer the spot to someone else and rebook you smoothly.
